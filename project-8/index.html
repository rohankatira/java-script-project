Apple Cinnamon Pancakes

These fluffy pancakes are infused with fresh apple chunks and warm cinnamon, creating a perfect balance of sweetness and spice. Served with maple syrup, they make for a comforting and delicious breakfast.

Chicken Biryani

This aromatic and flavorful rice dish is made with fragrant basmati rice, tender spiced chicken, and a blend of rich Indian spices, garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs. It is often served with raita (yogurt sauce) and is a staple in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisine.
